About me

I was born in Monrovia, CA and quickly learned that I wanted to see more of the world. I attended college at Kalamazoo College in Kalamazoo, Michigan. In my third year, I studied abroad in Japan and learned a lot about Japanese language and culture. After I graduated, I taught English in China at a Chinese university. Nine months later, I returned home and waited to start my next adventure-- teaching English in South Korea. I worked in Korea for thirteen months, while playing Ultimate Frisbee in tournaments around the country as well as neighboring countries.
